Q&A

  • What is a handfasting?

    A Handfasting is a Wiccan ceremony that binds a couple together. Traditionally, a couple would have a handfasting for a year and a day, after which a second handfasting would permanently seal their marriage.

  • What do I need to know to participate in the ceremony?

    Wiccan ceremonies are usually held in a circle. You can participate by focusing positive thoughts towards the couple when the circle is cast. Our circle will be created with incense powder which you may notice on the ground around the ceremony area. It is important not to disturb the incense powder and to not leave the circle once it is cast. If you must leave the circle, please exit at the back of the middle aisle where the gatekeeper will cut you out of the circle in a way that does not disturb the energy. There will also be opportunities to participate with important chants or spells.

  • How will I know the words to participate in chants or spells?

    The text of the ceremony will be provided in the program along with text and pronunciations for sections where attendees can participate. Chants will be sung through three times, first by the officiant alone and twice more by everyone.

  • What is the dress code?

    Formal, Black Tie Optional. No jeans please.
